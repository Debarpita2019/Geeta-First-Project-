Prathama Adhyaya Shlok 1

Dharmaksetre Kuruksetre Samaveta Yuyutsavah, Mamakah Pandavaschaiva Kim Kurvata Sanjaya?

Quick Translation - Dhritarashtra Asks: "O Sanjay, please tell me, what did my sons and Pandavas do after gathering near the holy battlefield of Kurukshetra with the desire to fight?
